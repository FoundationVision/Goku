Goku: Generative Flow Kit for Uniﬁed Image-Video Creation

Goku Foundation Team

High quality Text to Video Generation

Goku is a self-developed video generation model based on DIT architecture. It has self-developed 3D VideoVAE + Diffusion transformer architecture and can support the generation of 720P, 24FPS, and 4s high definition real videos.
Goku supports Text-to-Video generation, Image-to-Video generation, Text-to-image generation.

Abstract

This paper presents our latest advancements, Goku, a new family of joint image-and-video generation models based on rectiﬁed ﬂow Transformers to achieve industry-grade performance.

The Goku models achieve superior performance in both qualitative and quantitative assessments. Notably, Goku achieves top scores on major benchmarks: 0.76 on GenEval and 83.65 on DPG-Bench for text-to-image generation, alongside 84.8 on VBench for text-to-video tasks.

Vbench Performance

Goku-T2V attains an impressive score of 84.85 in VBench and has secured state-of-the-art performance on the leaderboard, surpassing several leading commercial text-to-video models.
